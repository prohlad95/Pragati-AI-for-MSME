# Judging Notes

## What Makes Pragati Strong

- Directly addresses the expected outcome: an AI/ML-driven MSME Financial Health Card.
- Uses the right alternate-data categories: GST, UPI, Account Aggregator, EPFO, and trade signals.
- Goes beyond prediction by including explainability, fairness, and expected-loss simulation.
- Is practical for banks because it improves both inclusion and portfolio quality.
- Is honest about synthetic data and production requirements.

## Recommended Demo Flow

1. Open the web app.
2. Show the Balanced MSME profile.
3. Switch to Growth and show how the score improves.
4. Switch to Thin File and explain why the app recommends review or defer instead of blind rejection.
5. Point to the explanation panel.
6. Point to the fairness dashboard.
7. Point to the expected-loss comparison.
8. Explain that the app is designed to plug into ULI/OCEN/AA rails.

## Suggested One-Minute Pitch

Pragati is an MSME Financial Health Card for credit-invisible businesses. Instead of relying only on formal financial documents, it aggregates consented alternate data such as GST behavior, UPI cash flows, Account Aggregator bank signals, EPFO payroll stability, and vendor payment discipline. It converts these signals into a health score, default probability, suggested credit band, and plain-language explanation. For banks, Pragati enables faster assessment, better portfolio quality, and fairer onboarding of viable New-to-Credit MSMEs. For borrowers, it creates a transparent path to credit readiness.
